Frequently Asked Questions

The alarm didn't go off — what should I do?

Make sure notification permissions are granted in Settings → SayItAlarm → Notifications. Also check that Do Not Disturb or Focus mode is not blocking Time Sensitive notifications.

Voice cloning or AI voice preview isn't working.

This feature requires an active internet connection. If it fails on the first try, wait a few seconds and try again — the voice server may be warming up.

How do I delete my cloned voice?

Open the alarm, tap the voice section, and remove the cloned voice. Your voice data on our servers is deleted automatically.

The alarm sound is the default tone instead of my voice.

This can happen if the voice audio couldn't be generated before the alarm fired (e.g. no internet at save time). Re-save the alarm while connected to the internet to regenerate the audio.
