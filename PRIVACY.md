# Privacy Policy — SayItAlarm

**Last updated: March 2026**

## Overview

SayItAlarm lets you set alarms that wake you with your own voice or a cloned AI voice. We collect only what is strictly necessary to provide this feature.

## Data We Collect

- **Voice recordings** — only when you choose to clone your voice. Your recording is sent to ElevenLabs (our AI voice provider) to create a voice model. We do not store the raw recording.
- **Alarm data** (time, label, repeat schedule) — stored locally on your device only.
- **Wake-up messages** — stored locally on your device only.

## How We Use Your Data

- Voice recordings are processed by ElevenLabs solely to generate your alarm audio. ElevenLabs' own privacy policy applies to this processing.
- Alarm data and messages never leave your device.
- We do not sell, share, or use your data for advertising.

## Third-Party Services

This app uses ElevenLabs for AI voice cloning. When you record your voice, it is transmitted to ElevenLabs' servers. Please review ElevenLabs' privacy policy at [elevenlabs.io](https://elevenlabs.io) for details on how they handle voice data.

No other third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs are used.

## Data Retention

- Cloned voice models are stored on ElevenLabs' servers under your app session. You can delete your cloned voice at any time from the Voice settings in the app.
- All other data remains on your device and is deleted when you uninstall the app.

## Your Rights

You may:
- Delete your cloned voice at any time from within the app.
- Delete all alarm data by uninstalling the app.
- Contact us to request deletion of any data held by ElevenLabs on your behalf.

## Children's Privacy

SayItAlarm is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
